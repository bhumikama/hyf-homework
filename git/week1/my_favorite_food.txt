Indian Recipe 1:
Aloo Jeera (Cumin Potatoes):

Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan and add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds. Let them sizzle.
Add 2 diced potatoes, 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Stir well.
Cover and cook on medium heat for about 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally until the potatoes are tender.
Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot. Enjoy!
